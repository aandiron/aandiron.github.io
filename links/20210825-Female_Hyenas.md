# Female Hyenas and Their Pseudopenises

Female spotted hyenas are more aggressive than males, socially dominant
over males, and 10% larger than males. They also have evolved so that
their clitoris is enlarged to form a fully erectile “pseudopenis” that
looks exactly like the male hyena’s penis. The females urinate copulate
and give birth through their pseudopenis. (During the birthing process
it’s ripped apart.) And for icing on their genital dessert, the females
also evolved their labia into a scrotal sac.
