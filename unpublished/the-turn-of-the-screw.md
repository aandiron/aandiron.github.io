## The Turn of the Screw
(With apologies to Henry James.)

Probably these days the term "screw" is a somewhat less popular
euphemism for fucking. I feel like "drill" is more commonly used, and
probably for most fucking more accurately captures the feeling of the 
action being executed. 

That is, I think most people in the act of fucking are doing a lot of
drilling and/or being drilled. There's a lot of in-out. There's a lot
of turning over. There's a lot of steady grinding. A hole is made and
desire is extracted from it.

Nothing wrong with that, I've been known to enjoy a good drilling
myself on occasion.

But with you, the person I lust for the most, I don't want to ever back
out. I don't want to churn, or wear-away, or pump you. I want to go only
one-way: inwards. I want it to be an inclined plane that drives it ever
forward, getting tighter and tighter as maximum depth is achieved. And
when it's there, when it is as tight as I can make it, I want to
stay there, fixed and set inside you. I want to be locked together
forever with you. No backing-out, no left-lucy, no gaping hole or
Spackle required.

You see what I'm saying? When it comes to you "screw" feels like the
right word. Other people might get drilled, but I want to screw you.
